  • *Pig Game , the one who first scores more than are equal to winning score is declared as winner
  • How to Play???
  • *Each player will get a chance one after one for rolling the dice(Roll Dice)
  • *There are two possibilities after rolling the dice, if the rolled dice value is one(1) ,then the chance for rolling the dice goes to opponent
  • *If we get other than one(1) then the dice value is added to current score and..
  • *Here, the interesting part,we can add the current score to total score by clicking on Hold the dice key, but we lose the chance to roll again..
  • *If you want roll again, then keep in mind we can roll any number of times without holding the dice but there is a chance you may get one (1) at anytime, then you will lose all the gained current score and also you lose your turn to roll the dice again
  • *It goes on until any of the player will score winning points
  • NOTE:At Present the winning points for game is 100, in future you will get an option to set the winning points(20,50,100,200,500,1000...)
